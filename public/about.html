About Me

I began my programming journey during my undergraduate years as a materials science student. I worked for the U.S. Air Force Materiel command in the Air Force Research Laboratory as a student researcher intern. I wrote countless (probably hundreds) of programs to run data analyses in MATLAB and Python. I absolutely hated data analysis coding projects.

I had to leave school in my junior year to care for my infant daughter who had multiple illnesses. After a year as a stay at home mom, I decided to learn web development as both a hobby and a means to continue my career in programming. I now live in suburban Ohio with my two daughters and husband.
